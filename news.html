MONKI preprint published in EGUsphere

The preprint “MONKI: a three-dimensional Monte Carlo simulator of total and polarised radiation reflected by planetary atmospheres” is available on EGUsphere. The paper presents the physical and numerical basis of the code and its validation.

2025 · EGU Vienna

Modelling Venus's spectropolarimetric signatures for EnVision

I presented work on adapting MONKI for Venus reflected-light simulations relevant to ESA's EnVision mission and the VenSpec instruments. This is part of my current postdoctoral work on Venus in total flux and polarisation.

2024 · Communications Earth & Environment

Solar eclipse cloud paper published

We showed that shallow cumulus clouds can dissipate quickly during solar eclipses as the land surface cools. The study used restored satellite observations to turn eclipses into natural experiments for cloud response.

Read the paper

2024 · SETI Institute

Interview on how solar eclipses affect clouds

I discussed the solar-eclipse cloud study and why temporary changes in sunlight can reveal how low clouds respond to surface cooling.

2022 · Public lecture

Searching for oceans on exoplanets

I gave a public astronomy lecture on how reflected light and polarisation can help reveal liquid-water oceans on planets around other stars.
